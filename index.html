Kyle William Brown

I am an accomplished Data Scientist with a background in scientific research who loves analyzing information and working with people in order to see the bigger picture. My robust data analytics skill set of Python (Pandas, Matplotlib), Microsoft Excel (VBA Macros), SQL, HTML, CSS, JavaScript (Plotly.js, D3.js, Leaflet.js) and Tableau and my Bachelor of Science in Earth and Space Exploration (Astrobiology and Biogeosciences) help me find the meaning behind large amounts of datasets. I completed the University of Arizona Data Analytics bootcamp and learned programming skills related to Python, SQL, R and JavaScript that help me analyze data more effectively.

I previously focused on projects concerning climate change and terrorism and now I want to expand toward more diverse science and business related positions. My technical skills would be a valuable contribution to any research analyst position because I would be able to programmatically analyze large amounts of information in order to come to a beneficial conclusion for both my employers and society as a whole.
